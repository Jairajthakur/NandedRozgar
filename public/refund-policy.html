Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

CityPlus is a platform for job listings, room rentals, vehicle listings, and buy & sell posts. This Refund Policy explains when you are eligible for a refund and how to request one.

✅ 1. When You Are Eligible for a Refund

A full refund is issued in the following situations:

  • Your listing was rejected by our moderation team before going live.
  • You were charged twice for the same listing due to a technical error.
  • A confirmed payment gateway error was recorded on our end.
✅ If any of the above applies, you will receive a full refund within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

🚫 2. When Refunds Are Not Issued

Payments are non-refundable in the following situations:

  • The listing has already gone live on the platform.
  • You changed your mind after the listing was published.
  • The listing expired at the end of its paid duration.
  • Your account was suspended due to a violation of our Terms of Service.
  • You applied a coupon code and later request a refund — the original amount (not the discounted amount) determines eligibility.
❌ Once a listing is live, no refund is issued regardless of the reason.

💰 3. Pricing & Plans

CityPlus offers the following paid listing options:

  • Job listings: ₹49 (7 days) · ₹79 (15 days) · ₹119 (30 days)
  • Room listings: ₹69 (15 days) · ₹99 (1 month) · ₹169 (2 months) · ₹229 (3 months)
  • Vehicle listings: ₹69 (15 days) · ₹99 (1 month) · ₹169 (2 months) · ₹229 (3 months)
  • Buy & Sell listings: ₹39 (7 days) · ₹59 (15 days) · ₹89 (30 days)

All prices are in Indian Rupees (₹) and include applicable taxes.

📋 4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us within 7 days of payment with the following details:

  • Your registered email address or phone number
  • The Cashfree order ID or transaction reference number
  • Reason for the refund request

Send your request to support@thecityplus.in or WhatsApp us at +91 98343 08805.

⏱️ 5. Refund Processing Time

Once a refund is approved:

  • Refunds are processed within 2 business days of approval.
  • The amount is credited to your original payment method within 5–7 business days, depending on your bank.
  • UPI refunds are typically faster (1–3 business days).

🏛️ 6. Disputes

If you believe a refund was incorrectly denied, you may escalate your case by emailing support@thecityplus.in with the subject line "Refund Dispute". We will review and respond within 3 business days. All disputes are governed by the laws of India and subject to the jurisdiction of courts in Nanded, Maharashtra.
